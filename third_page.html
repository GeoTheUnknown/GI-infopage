Aether/Lumine, also known as the Traveler and the Outlander, is the main protagonist and player character of Genshin Impact, as well as a cameo character in its accompanying webcomic series of the same name. Both them and their sibling were traveling out of Teyvat, but they were blocked by an Unknown God and were both defeated and sealed away. They were separated from each other and had their powers sealed away. While the Traveler's sibling eventually returned to Teyvat, the Traveler was sealed until 500 years later. Afterwards, the Traveler wandered around Teyvat until they came across Paimon, who helps guide the Traveler around Teyvat in search for their missing sibling.

Birthday: 9/9
Affliation: Wolvendom
Vision: Electro
Constellation: Lupus Minor
Height: 5'4" / 164 cm
Weapon: Claymore
Nation: Mondstadt

Razor was raised by Andrius, the Wolf of the North, and his wolf pack, after being abandoned as a baby. He is at home in the wild, although has recently started mixing a little more with Mondstadt's residents. He will fiercely protect his wolf pack family but does consider humans to be friends. He's protective and honest but lacks social graces due to his background.

Rosaria

Thorny Benevolence

Birthday: 1/24
Affliation: Church of Favonius
Vision: Cryo
Constellation: Spinea Corona
Height: 5'6" / 168cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Mondstadt

Rosaria is a sister in the Church of Favonius in Mondstadt, but you wouldn't realize unless you recognized her unique interpretation of a nun's attire. She's an unpredictable woman, often perceived as harsh and cold. If you're expecting to find her at choir practice then don't, she refuses to attend as she deems it "boring." Instead, she keeps her focus on the safety of the city, eliminating any threats she sees.

Sangonomiya Kokomi

Pearl of Wisdom

Birthday: 2/22
Affliation: Watatsumi Island
Vision: Hydro
Constellation: Dracaena Somnolenta
Height: 5'3" / 161cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Inazuma

Sangonomiya Kokomi is the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island in Inazuma. She's a descendent of the Sangonomiya clan and is currently leading the Sangonomiya Resistance. She is a skilled strategist and while not always directly involved in the every day current affairs of Watatsumi, she is actively helping fight the Vision Decree.

Sayu

Mujina Ninja

Birthday: 10/19
Affliation: Shuumatsuban
Vision: Anemo
Constellation: Nyctereutes Minor
Height: 4'6" / 138cm
Weapon: Claymore
Nation: Inazuma

Sayu is a young woman who is child-like in appearance due to her tiny height and delicate features. She is very strong thanks to her vision, which allows her to yield a Claymore, no easy feat for one so small. However, she is also very upset about her height and wishes to become taller. In an attempt to grow, Sayu often neglects her other duties as Shuumatsuban's resident ninja in favor of sleeping, believing this to be the answer to her problems. She has learned a number of ninjutsu techniques while trying to find ways to evade enemies and take a nap instead of fighting.

Shenhe

Lonesome Transcendence

Birthday: 3/10
Affliation: Cloud Retainer's Abode
Vision: Cryo
Constellation: Crista Doloris
Height: 5'5" / 166cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Liyue

Shenhe was born into an exorcist family, yet rather than being raised in this business, she was abandoned by her father, intended as a sacrifice. It is unclear how she survived against the monster she faced, yet she did. She would climb Mt. Hulao and watch the clouds before scavaging for sustenance in the area. This behavior caught the eye of Cloud Retainer, who offered her the chance to cut dies with the mortal world. Shenhe took advantage of this immediately, becoming a student of the Adepti. She has now been isolated for several years, causing her to become distant and indifferent.

Sucrose

Harmless Sweetie

Birthday: 11/26
Affliation: Knight of Favonius
Vision: Anemo
Constellation: Ampulla
Height: 5'1" / 156cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Mondstadt

Sucrose is Albedo's assistant and she specializes in bio-alchemy. She has an insatiable curiosity and seeks to enrich the world through science. During her time in Mondstadt she has created several interesting things, including jumbo Dandelions. She is usually found experimenting and meticulously documenting her results, which are often mixed in terms of success.

Tartaglia

Childe

Birthday: 7/20
Affliation: Fatui
Vision: Hydro
Constellation: Monoceros Caeli
Height: 5'11" / 182cm

Nation: Snezhnaya

Tartaglia is often known by his alias of Childe. He is the eleventh of the Eleven Harbingers of the Fatui and is considered one of the most dangerous. Childe is in charge of the Fatui's funding in Liyue and lives for chaos. He is very confident and brash, often appearing to be unpredictable and cunning.

Thoma

Protector From Afar

Birthday: 1/9
Affliation: Yashiro Commission
Vision: Pyro
Constellation: Rubeum Scutum
Height: 5'11" / 182 cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Inazuma

Thoma first become known to Traveler when they landed in Inazuma. As housekeeper to the Kamisato Clan, he is also a known 'fixer' of problems, as shown during the Archon quest chain. He is very friendly and fits in with any crowd he's part of. This approachability helps him carry out his duties. While he serves as Ayaka's help, she also considers him a friend. Thoma is very responsible and has a serious side, which compliments Ayaka's personality well, enabling the pair to work well together.

Tighnari

Verdant Strider

Birthday: 12/29
Affliation: Gandharva Ville
Vision: Dendro
Constellation: Vulpes Zerda
Height: 5'3" / 161cm
Weapon: Bow
Nation: Sumeru

Tighnari is a Forest Watcher from the Avidya Forest. He is steadfast and reliable, serving as a guide for those who are wandering, as well as a protector of the area. He's a very strong communicator and wishes to teach those he encounters about how to respect the forest. He joined the rangers as a graduate and saw a disorganised group with a good heart. He helped them become a more effective and cohesive organisation, protecting the area more efficiently that before.

Venti

Windborne Bard

Birthday: 6/16
Affliation: Mondstadt
Vision: Anemo
Constellation: Carmen Dei
Height: 5'3" / 161cm
Weapon: Bow
Nation: Mondstadt

Venti is a wine-loving bard who had the misfortune to appear underage in mortal form. He's actually Barbatos, the Anemo Archon, and is thousands of years old. He has a love for rhymes and playing the Lyre and has an incredible musical talent. It's this which has led him to become an incredibly popular bard, persuading people to purchase liquor for him in exchange for songs.

Wanderer

Eons Adrift

Birthday: 1/3
Affliation: None
Vision: Anemo
Constellation: Peregrinus
Height: 5'3" / 161cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Sumeru

Wanderer was formerly the sixth of eleven Fatui Harbingers, known by the codename Scaramouche. He previously appeared in the game in this form, but now wanders Sumeru amongst its people. This version of Scaramouche is much calmer and no longer a threat. His story is properly told through an in-game story quest that explores his journey for peace.

Mao Xiangling

Exquisite Delicacy

Birthday: 11/2
Affliation: Wanmin Restaurant
Vision: Pyro
Constellation: Trulla
Height: 5'2" / 158cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Liyue

Xiangling is a culinary prodigy. At the young age of 14 years old, Xiangling is the waitress and head chef of the family business, Wanmin Restaurant. She's known for going against the Liyue traditions of Li and Yue styles of cooking, instead using unique cooking ingredients and focusing on taste, rather than tradition.

Xiao

Vigilant Yaksha

Birthday: 4/17
Affliation: Liyue Adeptus
Vision: Anemo
Constellation: Alatus
Height: 5'2" / 158cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Liyue

Xiao is an adeptus and the only remaining Yaksha of the five sent to subdue demonic spirits in Liyue. His Yaksha name is Alatus but he currently lives a solitary life under the name Xiao at the Wangshu Inn. He is polite and reserved, preferring his own company to that of others. This is because he suffers constant pain due to negative karma gained in the line of his duties which he doesn't wish to inflict upon anyone else.

Vision: Hydro
Constellation: Fabulae
Height: 5'5" / 166cm
Weapon: Sword
Nation: Liyue

Xingqiu is the son of the manager of the Feiyun Commerce Guild but prefers books to commerce. He's polite, well-mannered, and often found reading or studying. Xinyan

Blazing Riff

Birthday: 10/16
Affliation: "The Red Strings"
Vision: Pyro
Constellation: Fila Ignium
Height: 5'2" / 158cm
Weapon: Claymore
Nation: Liyue

Xinyan is single-handedly trying to popularize rock 'n' roll in Liyue, a sound that hails from Fontaine. Music is in her soul and she frequently performs throughout Liyue Harbor. She's loud, defiant, passionate, rebellious, and everything else a great rockstar should be. Using a guitar she made herself she performs self-penned songs to anyone who will listen, bringing her sound to Liyue, one day at a time.

Yaoyao

Burgeoning Grace

Birthday: 3/6
Affliation: Streetward Rambler's
Vision: Dendro
Constellation: Osmanthus
Height: 4'6" / 138cm
Weapon: Polearm
Nation: Liyue

Yaoyao is the youngest disciple of the adeptus Streetward Rambler, more commonly known as Madame Ping. The pair act as if grandparent and grandchild, yet Yaoyao displays wisdom beyond her years under this tutelage. Talented and driven, Yaoyao often flips between mothering the other disciples, before betraying her youth through misunderstanding. Yaoyao is never without Yuegai, an adepti treasure she received from Cloud Retainer that protects her if she's in danger. All the adepti have a great affection for her and believe she has a bright future ahead.

Yae Miko

The Editor-In-Chief At The Yae Publishing House

Birthday: 6/27
Affliation: Grand Narukami Shrine
Vision: Electro
Constellation: Divina Vulpes
Height: 5'5" / 166cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Inazuma

Yae means Chief Priest and Miko is the Guuji of the Grand Narakami Shrine. We are unsure how long she has been in this role but is referred to by several Archons and described as an "old friend" of both Morax and Baal, suggesting that it is likely a long time. She is also one of Raiden Shogun's only friends, close enough for the pair to be on first name terms.We also see another side to Miko when she's running the Yae Publishing House. This feeds into her love of reading and literature in general, and being here lifts her spirits. She's a Kitsune and does have a fox form, but we have not seen this so far.

Yanfei

Wise Innocence

Birthday: 7/28
Affliation: Yanfei Legal Consultancy
Vision: Pyro
Constellation: Bestia Iustitia
Height: 5'2" / 158cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Liyue

Yanfei was born to a human mother and an Adeptus father, similarly to Ganyu. When she was grown her parents set off on an adventure, leaving her under the care of Madam Ping, who knew her father. Now living in Liyue, Yanfei has built a name for herself as a highly-skilled legal advisor. Yanfei's Adeptus heritage doesn't seem to affect her. She lives her life like other humans in the city and never entered into a contract with Rex Lapis, due to being born in peacetime. She is very intelligent and knowledgeable with a strong sense of justice. The law is her passion and she has learned as much as possible about it. While she specializes in Liyue law she does have legal knowledge that spans other regions of Teyvat as well. She takes her job very seriously, and even Ningguang respects her legal knowledge.

Yelan

Valley Orchid

Birthday: 4/20
Affliation: Yanshang Teahouse
Vision: Hydro
Constellation: Umbrabilis Orchis
Height: Wise Innocence
Weapon: Bow
Nation: Liyue

Yelan claims to work for the Ministry of Civil Affairs but is said to have taken over the operations in the Yangshang Teahouse on the side after investigating the previous owner. The Liyue "tea shop" has incredibly bad service, and is unaccessible by players. It is used as a front for a casino that Yelan now oversees. In public, Yelan is very pleasant and polite, yet maintains an air of secrecy. She still does some work for the Ministry and her intelligence is accurate enough that Ningguang seems to tolerate her absence, while Keqing appreciates any help she can get.

Birthday: 6/21
Affliation: Naganohara Fireworks
Vision: Pyro
Constellation: Carassius Auratkus
Height: 5'3" / 161cm

Constellation: Opera Grandis

Baizhu

Beyond Mortality

Birthday: 4/25
Affliation: Bubu Pharmacy
Vision: Dendro
Constellation: Lagenaria
Height: Height: 5'11" / 182cm
Weapon: Catalyst
Nation: Liyue

Biazhu is the distinctive-looking owner of Bubu Pharmacy in Liyue Harbor. He is known for being compassionate, skilled when it comes to solving health issues, and for wearing a white snake named Changsheng around his shoulders. His reputation is such that even the adeptus are aware of his knowledge and wisdom. Biazhu is also the guardian of Qiqi, having taken her in when he found her wandering in the hills, with no memory and difficulty performing basic tasks. Alongside his medical pursuits he is interested in understanding eternal life, something he got deeper into after Qiqi came into his life.

Kaveh

Empyrean Reflection

Birthday: 7/9
Affliation: Independent Design Studio
Vision: Dendro
Constellation: Paradisaea
Height: Height: 5'11" / 182 cm
Weapon: Claymore
Nation: Sumeru

Kaveh is a graduate from the Sumeru Akademiya known for being a talent architect and defender of the arts. He studied under the Kshahrewar Darshan and graduated with honors. However, during his time at the academy he built his masterpiece, the Palace of Alcazarzaray, which left him in a great deal of debt. In order to settle his debts, he lives with Alhaitham and the pair constantly bicker due to Kaveh getting flustered in Alhaitham's presence. Despite these arguments Kaveh is known as a thoughtful and kind young man, with a strong work ethic.
